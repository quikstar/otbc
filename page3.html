WHAT CAN I BRING INTO THE EVENT ?


Re-entry with admission wristband securely affixed to your wrist. No ripped, torn, or stretched wristbands permitted.

Low-back chair (one per person)

Sunscreen (protect yourself at Doheny State Beach)

Cell Phones/Tablets (remember to Facebook and Tweet while you’re at the festival)

Binoculars

Fanny packs

Small blanket or beach towel (one per person; max size 8’ x 8’)

Personal hand-held cameras (i.e. small digital or point-and- click cameras; no long/large/zoom/detachable lenses; no tripods; no pro cameras)

Umbrellas (must be set-up on outer perimeter- please do not block the view of other patrons)

Strollers (wagons and tyke bikes not permitted)

Unframed backpacks

Cigarettes/Lighters (please smoke in the designated smoking section – see site map)

Cash/Credit/ATM cards (re-entry will be allowed with your admission wristband)


WHAT SHOULD I LEAVE AT HOME ?


Illegal substances (no drugs of any kind)

Outside food or beverage

Alcohol (alcohol will be sold at the festival)

Knives/Weapons of any kind

Video cameras or video recording equipment. Video recording is prohibited.

High-back chairs

Glass/Glass Containers

Camelpacks/Bota Bags

Flags/Stickets/Flyers

Tents/Lawn Furniture/Large Tarps

Pets (exceptions made for service animals)

Professional or pro-sumer cameras and/or equipment (no large/long/zoom/detachable lenses or tripods)

Fireworks/Explosives/Laser Pointers

Skateboards/Scooters/Wagons/Motorized Vehicles

Bicycles inside the festival grounds. FREE Bike Valet is available outside the festival.

Illegal vending is not permitted. No unauthorized/unlicensed vendors allowed

Please note, all personal belongings will be searched prior to entrance, so please adhere to these rules so that others will not be delayed during the entrance process.
