 FAQ's

WHERE CAN I PURCHASE TICKETS?

You can purchase tickets online through our official festival ticketing site. If you have any questions you can email us at otbc@gmail.com . Mon. - Fri. from 10am to 5pm PST. If you have any technical or ticketing-related issues or questions, click here to contact Eventbrite 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

WILL THERE BE TICKETS SOLD AT THE EVENT?

Tickets will be sold at the event unless they run out prior to the event. If that occurs, we will notify customers in a newsletter sent by email. Add me to your e-Newsletter list to receive updates on artists, tickets, etc.

HOW MUCH ARE TICKETS DAY-OF- SHOW?

Day-of- Show tickets are $5 more expensive while supplies lasts, and there is no service fee if you purchase a ticket at the festival.
