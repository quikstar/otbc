VENUE

Old Town Front Street averages 28,500 vehicles per day. Old Town has a little bit of everything to offer including unique restaurants, boutique retail, hotels, museums, theater, art, antiques, farmer' s market, entertainment and over 40 annual special events including the famous Rod Run car show. There is executive and premier office space as well as mixed use and high-density residential creating highly desirable live/work environments.

The Temecula Civic Center is located in the heart of Old Town and includes a state of the art City Hall, Town Square event venue, 2,500 sq. ft. conference center, 480 space free public parking structure, police substation, and Convention & Visitors Bureau.

Vendor Information

Vendor Village

Peruse everything from hand-crafted art to hats and merchandise. Many of our event partners are on-site to spread the word about their companies. Make sure you stop by the Official Merchandise Booth – where you can take home some cool swag from the Temecula Blues Festival . But, get there early… almost everything sells out!

Food Vendors will have a variety of authentic vendors sampling everything from BBQ Ribs to Cajun Sausage to Vegetarian Pitas. Beverages of all types will be available, including beer, wine, cocktails, water and non-alcoholic beverages.
